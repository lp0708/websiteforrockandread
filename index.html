About Rock and Read

Rock and Read is a unique recommendation system that combines the joy of reading and listening to music while you're at it. Using the power of Google Books and Spotify APIs along with Google Cloud Services, we bring you tailored recommendations to suit your preferences.

Why Choose Us?

  • Curated book and song recommendations
  • Wide variety of genres and styles
  • Personalized suggestions for every mood

Services Used

Google Books API

Fetch book recommendations using the Google Books API tailored to your interests.

Spotify Web API

Discover music that complements your favorite books with the Spotify API.

Google Cloud Platform

Leverage GCP services for seamless backend integration and scalability.
